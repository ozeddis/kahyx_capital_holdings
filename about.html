Empowering Ambition with Purposeful Capital

Kahyx Capital Sàrl was established in 2019 in Perroy, Switzerland, as a culmination of over four decades of global project participation and equity investment. The company was formed to strategically manage and restructure the proceeds derived from a long-standing legacy of equity shareholdings across more than 140 international development projects, spanning a period of over 42 years.
Originally, compensation from these ventures was received largely in the form of project equity, rather than direct income. These equity positions were intentionally retained to maximize long-term asset growth within each project. With time, the founders initiated a carefully timed transition—relinquishing shareholdings and converting accumulated equity into liquid financial assets, including cash, stocks, bonds, and other diversified instruments.

This structured transformation was undertaken under the oversight and compliance of Deutsche Bank Frankfurt, whose officers issued formal confirmations of the fund's clean and verified origins. Through this process, Kahyx Capital solidified its foundation as a private investment entity with a strong financial base and a clear mandate: to provide long-term debt funding and financial solutions to businesses with strong growth potential.
Today, Kahyx Capital Sàrl leverages its capital strength, investment experience, and institutional credibility to deliver strategic, long-term financing that empowers businesses and drives sustainable impact across industries.
